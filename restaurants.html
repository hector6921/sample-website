1 Cabana latin fusion cuisine. You can find different flavors from the Caribean, Spain, south and central-America. Caribbean Cabana is a family owned and operated restaurant. Established in October of 2008. We specialize in West Indian cuisine and a fusion of Guyanese Chinese food. You are welcome to experience our family friendly restaurant area or our partitioned bar area. We pride ourselves on our delicious food and drinks along with our great staff and service. Our innovative specialty drinks are tasty and refreshing. Come and enjoy our happy hour in the evening or our house DJ for some nightlife activity.

2 Banter specializes in bringing a new twist to old Irish menu favorites. Instead of corned beef and cabbage, we have shredded Corned Beef and Cabbage Dumplings. Instead of white wine mussels, we create Guinness Mussels. Our aim is to bring the familiarity and warmth of an old Irish pub and mix it with modern food and cocktails. From our signature Magners Margaritas to our massive Banter Lamb Shanks, our goal is to have you planning your next trip here halfway through your meal.

3 Station House16 Craft Beer Taps, 25+ Bottles, 1 Cask, 120+ Whiskeys, Craft Cocktails, Progressive Pub Food, and an Expert Staff. Since our opening, we've been seeking to engage our clientele with hard-to-find, quality beer and whiskey. Our staff has been trained in the finer details of service and we seek to introduce our clientele to new beer, whiskeys, food, and cocktails so that they can share in our passion.

5 Agora Taverna features an extensive Greek seafood cuisine at modest prices. Enjoy us for Brunch 10:30 to 3:30pm on the weekends .
